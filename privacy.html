Privacy Policy

This page explains how information is handled on the Sai Silver Dum Biryani website.

Information We Collect

This website is primarily an informational landing page. It does not require account creation or direct payment on the site.

If you choose to contact the restaurant by phone, email, or external platforms such as Google Maps, Zomato, or Swiggy, your interaction is handled by those services or by the restaurant directly.

Reservation and Catering Forms

The current version of the website provides a front-end experience for reservation and catering requests. Form entries are not stored in a dedicated backend database by this static website alone.

External Services

Some links on this site open third-party platforms including Google Maps, Zomato, Swiggy, and Google Fonts. Those services may collect information according to their own privacy policies.

Cookies and Analytics

This static website does not intentionally use custom tracking cookies or analytics scripts. However, third-party services linked from the site may use their own tracking technologies.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, please contact Sai Silver Dum Biryani at contact@saisilverbiryani.com or call 089194 43535.
