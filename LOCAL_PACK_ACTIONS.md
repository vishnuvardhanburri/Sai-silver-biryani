# Local Pack Actions

These items cannot be changed from the website codebase alone and must be updated manually in the business platforms.

## 1. Brand Standardization

Use this exact name everywhere:

`Sai Silver Dum Biryani`

Update manually on:
- Google Business Profile / Google Maps
- Swiggy
- Zomato
- Instagram
- Facebook
- YouTube

Do not use variations like:
- Sai Silver
- Silver Dum
- Sai Dum Biryani

## 2. Google Business Profile

Complete these actions:
- Verify every branch or location profile
- Upload high-resolution menu photos with current prices
- Add food photos weekly
- Add short serving / plating videos
- Use Google Posts every week
- Promote `Sunday Mutton Special`
- Add service areas around Vijayawada
- Ensure phone, address, and hours exactly match the website

Welcome to Sai Silver Dum Biryani, widely recognized by locals as a destination for the best biryani in Vijayawada. Our Chicken Dum Biryani, Sunday Mutton Special, family pack biryani, takeaway service, and catering support make us a favorite for guests across Sanath Nagar, Auto Nagar, Benz Circle, and Mogalrajapuram.
