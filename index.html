Best Biryani in Vijayawada

Welcome to Sai Silver Dum Biryani, recognized by many local food lovers as a destination for the best biryani in Vijayawada. Our biryanis are prepared using a traditional pot dum process so the rice, masala, and meat slow-cook together for deeper flavor and a more authentic Andhra-style and Hyderabadi biryani experience.

Each grain of premium basmati rice carries aromatic spice and the richness of pure ghee. Our Sunday Mutton Special, family pack biryani, and signature non-veg combinations are favorites for guests coming from Sanath Nagar, Auto Nagar, Benz Circle, and Mogalrajapuram.
